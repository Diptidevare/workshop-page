Workshop

Arduino

We Conducted an exciting and interactive Arduino workshop, where participants explored the limitless possibilities of this versatile microcontroller. Designed to provide a solid foundation, the session began with an insightful overview of Arduino’s architecture, capabilities, and real-world applications. Participants were then introduced to the intuitive Arduino IDE, diving into its programming essentials and practical functionalities.Participants learned about its interfacing, programming, and integration with various electronic components and sensors, gaining hands-on experience to kickstart their journey in electronics and embedded systems.

Open CV

We conducted an exciting and hands-on OpenCV workshop, where participants explored the fundamentals of computer vision. The session covered key topics such as image reading and writing, applying various filters, and performing arithmetic and bitwise operations to manipulate images. Attendees learned about color spaces and color detection, followed by advanced techniques like face detection and object detection algorithms. With practical experience using OpenCV, participants gained valuable skills in building real-world applications in AI, machine learning, and robotics, making the workshop a great entry point for those interested in the rapidly growing field of computer vision.

PCB Designing

We conducted an engaging PCB Designing Workshop, where participants explored the essentials of creating circuits and designing PCBs using Proteus software. The workshop covered the entire PCB design process, from simulation and layout to manufacturing methods. Participants gained hands-on experience in soldering and desoldering, learning how to assemble and troubleshoot their own PCBs. By the end of the session, they were equipped with practical skills and knowledge, empowering them to confidently design and create circuits for future electronics projects. This workshop served as a perfect introduction to the world of electronics, embedded systems, and hardware development.

ROS

We conducted an engaging ROS Workshop that provided hands-on experience with the Robot Operating System (ROS), guiding participants through the setup of a Virtual Machine (VM) and ROS installation. The workshop covered key concepts like ROS nodes, publishers and subscribers, and talker-listener applications for node communication. Participants also explored Turtlesim for visualizing robot movement, learned about ROS topics, and used RViz for 3D visualization of robot states and sensor data. The session concluded with a practical exploration of the E-Yantra robot on Gazebo, simulating real-world robot tasks in a virtual environment. By the end, attendees were equipped with essential ROS skills to innovate in robotics and automation.

CAD & 3D Printing

Our CAD & 3D Printing Workshop offered an immersive hands-on experience with SolidWorks, covering everything from installation and tool design to sketch features, assembly design, and static simulation. Participants learned how to create and assemble components efficiently, testing designs under real-world conditions. The workshop also featured an exciting Speed CAD Challenge, where attendees competed to complete design tasks under time pressure, honing their creativity and problem-solving skills. This workshop equipped participants with valuable skills in 3D modeling, product design, and rapid prototyping, essential for success in modern engineering and design fields.

Raspberry Pi

We hosted an engaging Raspberry Pi workshop, designed to introduce participants to the power of this versatile single-board computer. The session covered key topics such as Raspberry Pi pinouts, IDE setup, and the boot-up process, providing a solid foundation for beginners. Participants gained hands-on experience interfacing with various sensors and outputs, learning how to control devices like motors, LEDs, and sensors through GPIO pins. This workshop was the perfect opportunity to explore the world of IoT, home automation, robotics, and embedded systems, equipping attendees with the skills and confidence to create innovative projects using Raspberry Pi.
